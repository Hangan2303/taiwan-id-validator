National ID · Unified Business Number

National ID (中華民國國民身分證): 1 letter + 9 digits · D1 = 1 (male) / 2 (female) · Modulo 10 checksum

Unified Business Number (統一編號): 8 digits · Weights [1,2,1,2,1,2,4,1] · Modulo 5 checksum

D7 = 7 exception: 7×4=28→10, treated as 1 or 0 — either sum passing modulo 5 makes the number valid.

Generates a random 8-digit number that passes the modulo-5 checksum. When the 7th digit is 7, both sum paths are validated.
